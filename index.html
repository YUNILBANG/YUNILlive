The Intersection of Structure and Improvisation
This work reveals the instability of communication within the fluid movements of visuals and sound, guiding the audience toward sensory immersion. It explores the tension between spontaneity and unexpected variables that arise within the framework of a predefined system. By embracing real-time data fluctuations and technical variables as essential elements, the project archives a fleeting experience where intentional control and improvised freedom collide and converge.
